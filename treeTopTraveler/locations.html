Locations

United States

Choose from several different locations in the USA for tree house rentals that feature rugged adventure or rustic charm.

Hunter's Lodge, Montana, USA

If you are looking to bag that 5-point buck or simply need a place to fight off the chill this multi-tier tree house has room for the whole family with full accommodations. K-9 companion’s welcome!

Rustic Mountain Hideaway, North Carolina, USA

Aspiring authors will love this treehouse! It is the perfect space to knock out that writer’s block and create the next big novel for the New York Times best seller’s list. With stunning panoramic views from every room in this two story 4-bedroom treehouse, you will not be at a loss for inspiration. It comes fully equipped with power, heat, kitchen, and bath.

Spain

Crow’s Nest bungalow, Bizkaia, Spain

Come enjoy the view from this apartment bungalow nestled in the wilderness of Bizkaia, Spain. It features a full-service of accommodations including kitchen, bathroom, and fireplace to keep you warm on cold nights.

Indonesia

Beach Island Getaway, Bali, Indonesia

If you are more interested sunnier climes, this cozy apartment is perfect for a young couple out on their honeymoon, or a pair of empty nesters out for adventure after their flock has left the nest. It lies just off the beach and features full accommodations including air conditioning, kitchen, bed, bath, and wi-fi.
