Overview

Purpose

When you were a child, did you ever want a treehouse in your backyard? Whether you had one or not, treehouses represent childhood freedom, exploration, and imagination. I want to reintroduce these feelings to the adventurer in all of us, both young and old. My website treetoptraveler.com provides people with the opportunity to travel all over the world to stay in amazing and beautiful treehouses. The process is easy, you simply visit the website and search for a treehouse, based on your needs, like location or style. Once booked, you will be able to experience the vacation of your childhood dreams, while still enjoying the modern comforts of a hotel or bed \& breakfast.

Audience

Here at Tree Top Traveler our business is catered to the adventurous inner child in all of us, but that’s not all. If you aren’t interested in roughing it, our accommodations will meet all the needs of your modern sensibilities. Our treehouses come equipped with fully functional bathrooms, a kitchen for meal preparation, central heating, air conditioning, electricity, even room service; if that’s what you want, but if you’re more rough and tumble, we’ve got you covered as well. If you are adventurous campers, a newlywed couple, empty nesters, or a family still welcoming new additions, check out Tree Top Travelers and sleep out under the stars – you won’t be disappointed!

Typography

Heading Font: Amatic SC

I think the look of this font will match well with the theme of my site. It has clean lines that are largely straight, but have slight curves to them, which reminds me of the way that most tree trunks grow. It appears somewhat on the small side, so I may have to use larger font to get the desired look I want.

Paragraph Font: Cabin

I chose this font because it is very readable at the smaller size of a paragraph. It is a sans-serif font with nice wide letters and clean lines.

Home page

Tree Top Traveler

We provide tree house rentals for Business, Rest, Relaxation, Enjoyment, Culture, Whimsey, Food, Fancy, Fun… and so much more. Come and see what we have to offer.

Our tree houses range from single room studio apartments to multi-tiered, penthouse villas on stilts. Hey, we could probably argue that every listing we offer comes as a penthouse suite, but we won’t. What we will say, is that if you decide to stay with us, no matter which of our listings you may choose to stay in, you are guaranteed fresh air, peaceful surroundings, and inspiring scenic views.

Philosophy

Tree Top Traveler was started by a group of friends who wanted to provide customers with comfortable accommodations from any location in the world – provided there are trees nearby. However, they felt like the typical room and board situation had been so overused by the motel, hotel, and bnb crowd that it was time to change things up, which is why they decided that Tree Top Traveler would specialize in providing its customers with convenient, modern, affordable tree house rentals to meet their varied needs for travel and adventure. Thus, Tree Top Traveler was born.

Operations

While Tree Top Traveler does maintain and operate many tree house properties across the United States, we have also partnered with professionals and private citizens to offer similar accommodations from varied locations throughout the world. These partnerships ensure that you can expect to enjoy the same level of professionalism and care from any location that is offered by Tree Top Traveler.

Safety

Before any tree house property is listed on Tree Top Traveler, it goes through a series of rigorous inspections to ensure that it meets the safety standards required by local, national, and state building codes, as well Tree Top Traveler’s own extensive safety rules established and patterned after the US Housing and Securities Board. If you have any concerns, our staff is available on our website 24/7 to meet your needs, and answer your questions regarding the safety of properties operated by ourselves or any of our partners.
